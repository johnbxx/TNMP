Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

What This App Does

Are the Pairings Up? is an independent tool that checks whether pairings have been posted for the Tuesday Night Marathon chess tournament at the Mechanics' Institute in San Francisco. It can also notify you via push notifications or SMS when pairings or results are posted.

Data We Collect

Player Name

If you enter your name in Settings, it is stored in your browser's local storage only. It is used to find your pairing in the tournament data. Your name is also sent to our server if you subscribe to SMS notifications, so we can include your pairing details in the text message.

Phone Number (SMS subscribers only)

When you subscribe to SMS notifications, your phone number is:

  • Sent to our server over HTTPS
  • Normalized to standard format and immediately encrypted using AES-256-GCM
  • Stored in Cloudflare KV with a SHA-256 hash as the key — the plaintext number is never stored
  • Decrypted only when sending you an SMS notification

On your device, only a SHA-256 hash of your phone number is stored in local storage — never the number itself.

Push Notification Endpoint (push subscribers only)

If you enable push notifications, your browser generates a push subscription endpoint URL (managed by your browser vendor, e.g. Google or Apple). This URL is stored on our server and used solely to deliver notifications. It does not contain personal information. Your notification preferences (pairings/results) and player name are stored alongside the endpoint.

IP Address

Your IP address is used solely for rate limiting API requests. It is stored temporarily with an automatic 5-minute expiration. It is not logged or retained beyond that.

Third-Party Services

SMS messages are delivered through Twilio. Your phone number is shared with Twilio solely for message delivery. See Twilio's Privacy Policy.

This app is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and Workers. Subscriber data is stored in Cloudflare KV (encrypted as described above). See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Cookies and Tracking

This app uses no cookies, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind. There are no third-party scripts, advertising, or data brokers.

Data Retention

SMS subscriber records are retained until you unsubscribe. Push subscription records are retained until you disable push notifications or your browser revokes the subscription. Rate limit records expire automatically after 5 minutes. Browser local storage persists until you clear it or unsubscribe.

Your Rights

  • Unsubscribe from SMS or disable push notifications at any time via Settings. This permanently deletes your server-side subscriber record.
  • Clear local data by clearing your browser's site data for tnmpairings.com, or by removing your name in Settings.
