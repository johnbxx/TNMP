Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

What This App Does

Are the Pairings Up? is an independent tool that checks whether pairings have been posted for the Tuesday Night Marathon chess tournament at the Mechanics' Institute in San Francisco. It can also notify you via push notifications when pairings or results are posted.

Data We Collect

Player Name

If you enter your name in Settings, it is stored in your browser's local storage only. It is used to find your pairing in the tournament data. If you enable push notifications, your name is also sent to our server so we can include your pairing details in the notification.

Push Notification Endpoint (push subscribers only)

If you enable push notifications, your browser generates a push subscription endpoint URL (managed by your browser vendor, e.g. Google or Apple). This URL is stored on our server and used solely to deliver notifications. It does not contain personal information. Your notification preferences (pairings/results) and player name are stored alongside the endpoint.

IP Address

Your IP address is used solely for rate limiting API requests. It is stored temporarily with an automatic 5-minute expiration. It is not logged or retained beyond that.

Third-Party Services

This app is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and Workers. Push subscription data is stored in Cloudflare KV. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Cookies and Tracking

This app uses no cookies, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind. There are no third-party scripts, advertising, or data brokers.

Data Retention

Push subscription records are retained until you disable push notifications or your browser revokes the subscription. Rate limit records expire automatically after 5 minutes. Browser local storage persists until you clear it.

Your Rights

  • Disable push notifications at any time via Settings. This permanently deletes your server-side subscription record.
  • Clear local data by clearing your browser's site data for tnmpairings.com, or by removing your name in Settings.
